Hi!

I'm Manasvini.

(pronounced muh-nuhs-vin-ee)

I started studying and practicing law.

I studied law in the University of Oxford, and National Law Institute University.

After graduating from Oxford, I worked for a few years in a corporate law firm, dealing mostly with transactional law, contracts, and private equity.

Then I learned to code.

I started to learn Python, and then Django. My learning really accelerated when I started a challenge to make one project a day, for a month. One month challenges are my favourite way to learn.

This also started my journey as a maker, and since, I've created several products, reaching thousands of people.

I created Lawbot, an automated legal contract reviewing engine.

Lawbot identifies problematic issues in contractual language, and suggests ways to fix them.

Then I made Serendipity. It introduced you to one (random) awesome person every month.

This was such a fun project to work on, and it became hugely popular. The day I launched on Product Hunt, it was voted to be the #1 project for the day.

It also got nominated for Product Hunt's Tech Product of the Year.

Over 6000 people used Serendipity, and we made hundreds of wonderful, weird, serendipitous connections.

In 2018, I launched Boss as a Service.

Boss as a Service is a productivity service / tool, which gives you a "Boss" -- a real human being who chases you up to make sure you're on track to achieving your goals. If you've ever made a New Year's Resolution and promptly forgotten about it, you know what I mean.

Boss as a Service went to #1 on Hacker News, and was featured on Product Hunt, and I participated in an interview on CBC.CA's podcast.

I also made Why Do I Procrastinate, the Web MD for Procrastination.

Why Do I Procrastinate started as a side project for Boss as a Service. It diagnoses the reason why you're not doing whatever it is you're supposed to be doing. It also got to #1 on Hacker News!
